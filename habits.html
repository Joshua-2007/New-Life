Habits

  1. Practice Voice Excerises
    Why? Because i want to speak openly and confidently with my natural voice.

  2. Body and Posture Awareness
    Why? Because it helps me relax my body's tension and so i can learn to listen to what my body is telling me.

  3. Breath Awareness and Deep Breathing
    Why? Because it brings me back to the present moment and helps keep the emotions in balance.

  4. Develop Concentration
    Why? Because we live in a world full of distractions and concentration is one of the most valuable skills to develop to succeed in life.

  5. Emotional State Awareness
    Why? Because emotions are the driving force behind all my actions and becoming aware of the emotions is the first step behind mastering them.

  6. Drinking 8 glasses of Water Per Day
    Why? Because keeping hydrated is crucial for health and well-being.

  7. Cultivate a State of Abundance
    Why? Because scarcity mindset is the source of all suffering and I want to live a happy life.
